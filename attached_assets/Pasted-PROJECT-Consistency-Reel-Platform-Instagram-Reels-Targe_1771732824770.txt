PROJECT: Consistency Reel
Platform: Instagram Reels
Target Duration: 25–30 sec
Primary Goal: Authority + Follower Growth

Core Objective

This video should position you as someone who understands discipline and productivity deeply. The goal is to trigger self-reflection and drive follows.

Target Audience:
18–28 year olds struggling with consistency.

Desired Emotion:
Slight discomfort → realization → motivation.

CTA:
“Follow for practical discipline systems.”

Hook Strategy (0–3 sec)

Hook Style: Bold statement

Suggested Opening Line Options:

“You don’t lack motivation. You lack structure.”

“Consistency isn’t hard. You’re just doing it wrong.”

“Stop blaming motivation.”

Execution:

Immediate hard cut start (no intro)

Bold center caption (white text, yellow keyword highlight)

Slight punch-in zoom at 0:01

Subtle bass drop or impact sound at hook

Structure Timeline Plan

0–3 sec
Pattern interrupt + bold statement.

3–15 sec
Explain the real problem:
Most people rely on motivation instead of systems.

15–23 sec
Give a clear actionable insight:
Lower friction. Make habits stupidly easy.

23–28 sec
Reinforce identity shift:
“Discipline is engineered, not felt.”

28–30 sec
CTA:
“Follow for systems that actually work.”

Editing Style

Overall Vibe:
Iman Gadzhi pacing with Ali Abdaal clarity.

Pacing:

Cut every 2–3 seconds

Remove pauses longer than 0.5 sec

Keep momentum tight

Camera Movement:

Subtle punch-in zoom at emphasis words

No aggressive motion

Transitions:

Hard cuts only

No flashy transitions

Color Grade:

Slightly darker contrast

Warm skin tone

Clean background

Caption Strategy

Style:

Bold center captions

Word-by-word animation

Highlight key words in yellow

Density:

80% of spoken words captioned

Emphasis on emotionally charged phrases

Safe zone compliance for Instagram:

Keep captions above lower 20% of frame.

Audio & Music

Music Type:

Subtle motivational instrumental

Low volume under voice

Music Drop:

Light drop at hook

Sound Effects:

Subtle whoosh on transitions (optional)

Impact sound at hook line

B-Roll Suggestions

Minimal usage.

Optional insert:

0:12: Short stock clip of someone journaling

0:18: Close-up typing on laptop

Do not overuse B-roll.
Maintain authority tone.

Technical Checklist for Editor

Trim all filler words.

Remove pauses > 0.5 sec.

Normalize audio levels.

Add light compression for clarity.

Ensure total duration < 30 sec.
